FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is LexBot providing actual legal advice?
+
No. LexBot provides legal information only — it helps you understand laws, procedures, and your rights in plain language. For professional legal advice specific to your case, please consult a licensed advocate or lawyer. LexBot is an educational tool, not a substitute for professional legal counsel.
What kinds of legal questions can I ask?
+
You can ask about FIR filing procedures, cyber crime complaints, consumer rights, Right to Information (RTI), domestic violence laws, bail processes, labour rights, property disputes, and many more common legal topics under Indian law. The more specific your question, the better the answer.
Is my chat data private and secure?
+
Yes. Your conversations with LexBot are stored securely and are not shared with any third parties. Chat history is saved only to help you review past information. You can download or delete your chat history at any time. We do not store personally identifiable information.
Does LexBot support Hindi?
+
Yes! LexBot supports both English and Hindi. You can toggle between languages using the EN/HI button in the navigation bar. The interface and response content will switch accordingly, making legal information accessible to Hindi-speaking users as well.
Can I use voice input to ask questions?
+
Absolutely! Click the 🎤 microphone button in the chat input area to use voice input. LexBot uses the Web Speech API to convert your spoken words into text. This feature works in modern browsers like Chrome, Edge, and Safari. Make sure to allow microphone permissions when prompted.
How accurate is the information provided?
+
LexBot's information is based on Indian law including the IPC, CrPC, Consumer Protection Act, IT Act, and other relevant legislation. While we strive for accuracy, laws can change and interpretations may vary. Always verify important information with an official government source or a qualified legal professional before taking action.
